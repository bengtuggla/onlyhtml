Alicante

The best way to go to Costa Blanca is by flight.
Lots of airline companies fly to Alicante airport .


Airport bus

There are lots of buses, just outside the terminal, departing to all of Costa Blanca.
Some buses goes to Alicante city and the bus terminal.


Hotels

Prebooking your hotel is needed because almost every room
in Costa Blanca is occupied.


Top 5 Tourist Attractions

Here are the five most visited attractions in Costa Blanca:

  1. BENIDORM
    • A sea of glittering skyscrapers lines
      five perfect kilometers of golden sand.

  2. AQUALANDIA
    • Aqualandia claims to be Europe's biggest
      water park, and it's huge.

  3. GUADALEST VALLEY
    • A really beautiful little village in the mountains.
      Lots of little shops, museums and cafés.

  4. CALPE
    • It’s pleasant walking along the promenade especially
      in the direction of the Rock which makes a great backdrop.

  5. SANTA BARBARA CASTLE Castle
    • You can't fail to notice this impressive castle situated
      on an outcrop over looking Alicante City.
